Add to List

Here are the requirements and instructions to add your Cytube room to the Adult list.

Requirements:

  • Add MOTD to room
  • 18+

MOTD:

  • Add the following to "Channel Settings->Edit->MOTD". Then click "Save MOTD" to comply with CyTu.be "Terms of Use". Replacing {ROOM-NAME} with whatever you want to call the room but include "18+". A list of colors can be found here: https://htmlcolorcodes.com/color-names

{ROOM-NAME} 18+
